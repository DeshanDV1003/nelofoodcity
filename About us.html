About Us------------ 


At Nelo Food Corner, we are passionate about providing you with a seamless shopping experience and offering a wide range of high-quality products to meet all your needs. With years of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted destination for grocery shopping, catering to the diverse preferences of our valued customers.

Our well-stocked shelves showcase an extensive selection of fresh produce, pantry staples, household essentials, and more, sourced from reputable suppliers. Our dedicated team of friendly and knowledgeable staff members is always ready to assist you, ensuring that your visit to our store is both convenient and enjoyable. We take pride in our commitment to exceptional customer service, competitive prices, and a clean, organized shopping environment. Whether you're searching for everyday groceries or unique specialty items, Nelo Food Corner is here to make your shopping experience convenient, affordable, and memorable.

Shop with us and discover the difference of a supermarket that truly values its customers.

Vision
To create a thriving and sustainable food city chain that enriches the lives of our customers, fosters local economies, promotes healthy and responsible food choices, and celebrates culinary diversity. 
Mission 
Our mission is to provide an exceptional dining and shopping experience within our food city chain, offering a wide range of high-quality and ethically sourced food products from local farmers, artisans, and suppliers. We aim to be the preferred destination for food enthusiasts, catering to their diverse tastes and dietary preferences. Through our commitment to sustainability, we strive to minimize our environmental impact by promoting eco-friendly practices and reducing food waste throughout our operations. By actively engaging with local communities, we seek to support and empower small-scale producers, contributing to the economic development of the regions we serve. Ultimately, we aspire to inspire a passion for good food and foster a culture of conscious consumption, promoting healthier lifestyles and a deeper appreciation for the culinary arts.
